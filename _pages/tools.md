Small things I've built for researchers — playful, self-contained, no accounts.

## Research Values — *Would You Rather*

A forced-choice game that surfaces your **research values**. Pick the fate you'd *rather* — and
watch a picture of what you actually trade for take shape. Two modes: a scored questionnaire that
maps your choices onto ten research trade-offs (curiosity vs real-world impact, rigor vs novelty,
depth vs breadth, autonomy vs security, integrity vs expedience, and more) into a research-values
fingerprint; and a lightweight **icebreaker** for lab retreats and seminars. There are no wrong
answers — the reasoning is the point. Everything runs in your browser; nothing is sent anywhere.
